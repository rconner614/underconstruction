Hondros Learning | eCommerce Portal

Working for Hondros, I was tasked with redesigning their eCommerce / eLearning websites. Hondros Learning teams up with resellers to sell Real Estate, Appraisal, Mortgage and Insurance courses around the country. When they pair with a school, a "portal" is created for students to buy from directly. My goal was to update the look of all these portals.

Each portal uses the same base code and dynamically pulls in styling, products, and account information based on the domain. The site utilizes AngularJS, Bootstrap, HTML5 and runs on a nodejs server. Our development team at Hondros continues to work on this site to make it faster and powerful, while I strive to make the user experience the best it can be.

Raise the Bar | CSS Graphic

While working for Hondros, my boss challenged me to make a landing page site in less than a week as a learning opportunity, which I did in my free time. That was the first time I'd ever made anything as a professional and I didn't make it easy on myself. I wanted a large typographical piece to be the highlight of the page, and I did it purely with CSS and HTML. All that survives today is the graphic, but it was what I liked best about the project anyway. The best [and hardest part] of the project was making it responsive. But when it was done, it looked great no matter what you viewed it with.

J. Paulette Forshey | Author Website

After making book covers for author J. Paulette Forshey, I created a website concept to update her current site.

Cutting her website costs drastically, I setup a free Wordpress blog using the styles I'd created in this mockup.
